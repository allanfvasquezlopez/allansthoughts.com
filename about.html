about

Musician, composer, and storyteller Allán (ah-YAHN) Vasquez-Lopez thrives on exploring the worlds of new music and folk music, creating new worlds in the process by intersecting queer and latinx identities through composition and performance. Allán has received a degree from Santa Monica College and is presently attending the California Institute of the Arts (CalArts). Currently, Allán is a member of Mariachi Arcoiris de Los Angeles, the world’s first LGBTQ+ mariachi, as well as an active musician and composer in the CalArts community.
